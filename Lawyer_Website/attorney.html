William A. Kennedy

After serving four years in the United States Army, Mr. Kennedy attended San Diego State University, earning a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology with Honors. Mr. Kennedy then attended Washburn University in Topeka, Kansas, receiving a Juris Doctorate Degree in 2003. After receiving his law degree, Mr. Kennedy moved to Las Vegas, and practiced with a large insurance defense firm for several years. In 2006 Mr. Kennedy opened the Kennedy Law Firm, and since that time Mr. Kennedy has been zealously representing clients in both Civil and Criminal matters, before all Courts in the State of Nevada.

The United States Army instilled in Mr. Kennedy a determined sense of honor and commitment, which continues to drive his pursuit of excellent representation for his clients. A combination of experience, client-centered approach, and consistent efforts has earned Mr. Kennedy an excellent reputation in the legal community. Professional
